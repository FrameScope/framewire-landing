About

A contextual intelligence platform that helps people understand evolving information environments — not just consume content.

FrameWire continuously collects and processes information from news portals, social media, and open-source intelligence sources. Through AI-assisted narrative, sentiment, emotion, fact-check, and threat analysis, it reveals how narratives spread, evolve, and shape public perception.

Unlike traditional dashboards, it runs on a shared intelligence memory — where information, relationships, and contextual signals connect and accumulate over time. Multilingual processing, entity extraction, semantic clustering, and timeline reconstruction turn fragmented data into operational understanding.

Everything is built around explainable intelligence: you can inspect the evidence, verify sources, and see how conclusions were formed. AI assists the investigation, but judgment always stays with you.

Built for journalists, researchers, investigators, institutions, and analysts who need deeper situational awareness — FrameWire doesn't tell you what to think. It helps you investigate, compare, verify, and understand complex realities with greater clarity.

— FrameWire Team
